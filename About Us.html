ABOUT US

STORY

Oro Cultura is a creative event curation company based in Manila that focuses on celebrating local talent and culture through bespoke pop-up events. We specialize in crafting immersive experiences that showcase the best of Filipino music, art, and culinary excellence.

MISSION

High-quality pop-up events that bring people together through the magic of local music, art, and food, while celebrating and providing a platform for Filipino artists. We strive to create unforgettable, immersive experiences that highlight the rich cultural heritage and creativity of the Filipino.

VISION

To become the leading cultural event curator in Manila, recognized for elevating creativity, craftsmanship, and the voices of local artists in every event we produce. We aim to contribute to the growth and global recognition of Manila's vibrant art, music, and culinary scene, fostering a thriving community where local talent can shine.
